Stability without Stagnation

In the fast moving and ever changing frontend world, Ember.js is the stable foundation to build on for the long term. While leading technological innovation in many areas, it emphasizes stability and backwards-compatibility and supports projects that are built to last.

Community Engagement

Ember.js is a project built by an open community and simplabs is an active member of that community. We support the Ember.js Munich and Ember.js Berlin meetups as well as EmberFest, the european Ember Community Conference.
