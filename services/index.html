Our Services

We do Software Engineering, Technology Consulting as well as Individual and Group Trainings.

Software Engineering

simplabs builds solutions for the web and mobile devices for everyone from small startups to big enterprises. Our solutions are well architected and easy to maintain and extend by our clients once we have left.

We turn our client's ideas into successful products by taking over projects completely and working closely with the client's Product Team in short iterations.

For clients that have in-house developers already, we can augment that existing team to help reach delivery deadlines or improve quality. By working with our experienced experts, the client's developers will be able to extend and deepen their knowledge in the respective technologies.

Technology Consulting

We offer consulting services to help clients successfully implement their own projects based on decisions and designs that we help make and validate.

Having years of experience with Ruby on Rails, Ember.js, distributed systems, scaling of large applications and related technologies, we can for example help find performance bottlenecks in existing applications, review architectures or design complex client server systems.

Trainings

simplabs offers individual and group training by tutors with extensive experience in the respective technologies. From introductory workshops on frameworks like Ember.js or Phoenix to advanced classes on distributed systems design, authentication, deployment or scalability, we set up customized training sessions that suit the needs of clients and their teams.
