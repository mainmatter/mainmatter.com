About

We are an international team of Web and Open Source enthusiasts.

Values

simplabs is a technology focused consultancy with an international team. We help clients across the world by applying and sharing our expertise.

We Open Source and take an active role in the projects that we leverage for our clients, contributing code and supporting the communities.

Expertise over Location

We value expertise and a passion for technology higher than location. Our distributed team of technology experts works for clients across the world.

Having a remote culture doesn't mean the team needs to be distributed all the time though. We have regular company events to connect offline and spend some time together - we also invite our clients to join us!
