About

We are Open Source enthusiasts from Munich, Germany.

Bavarian Engineering

simplabs is located in Munich, Germany and works for clients all over the world. We offer software engineering, technology consulting and training with web and mobile technologies. If we cannot provide something ourselves, we have a network of trusted engineers and designers that we work with.

simplabs Open Source

simplabs loves Open Source and we don't only use it to implement our client's projects but also actively maintain a number of projects ourselves.

Using open source ensures our projects are based on a solid foundation and benefit from the community behind it.

Community Engagement

We sponsor conferences like EmberConf and RubyDay.it, organize the Munich Ember.js Meetup and help girls and women learn Rails at Rails Girls.

Reaching out to the community is the best way to move open source forward, which eventually benefits everyone.
