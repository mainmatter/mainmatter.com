How can a web mobile app help Trainline leverage their full market potential?

Services

Mobile Web App Development

Trainline is Europe's leading independent rail and coach platform. They sell tickets to customers worldwide, enabling more than 125,000 journeys every single day in and across 36 countries, covering 150 different carriers.

Simplabs worked closely with Trainline’s in-house engineering team to deliver an app-level experience for their mobile users. A dedicated site for mobile devices, allowed Trainline's customers to benefit from the best of website performance, without the need to install an app —they could now simply navigate to the site on any browser.

At simplabs, we believe that both end-users and business can benefit from the advantages provided by Progressive Web Applications, which is why we recommend them as the better choice for our clients looking to provide seamless, engaging experiences to their mobile users.

To learn more about the impact a Progressive Web App could have on your mobile experience and engagement, talk to one of our technology experts.
