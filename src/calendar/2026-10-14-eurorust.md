EuroRust is a four-day conference for the European Rust community. It covers all things Rust: from Rust patterns and idioms to system programming and CLI tooling, servers WASM and embedded systems. Mainmatter hosts EuroRust and our team will be in Barcelona in full force this year to run the event as well as deliver talks and workshops.
